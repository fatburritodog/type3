    • Terminology

    IMPERSONATION - to assume the character or appearance of someone else.

    OUTING - disclosing the sexuality of an LGTBQ sexual or gender identity without consent.

    CYBERSTALKING - the use of theInternet or other online electronics to stalk or harass either an individual, group, or organization. False accusations, monitoring, threats, identity theft, data destruction, manipulation, vandalism, or soliciting sex. It is often times accompanied by unwanted real life stalking as well.

    DOXING - the Internet based practice of researching and broadcasting private and identifiable information (such as home addresses, work numbers, phone numbers, etc). Much of the research is gathered through public social media website

    FLAMING - a hostile and insulting interaction between persons over the internet, often involving the use of profanity. It can also be the swapping of insults back and forth or with many people teaming up on a single victim.

    TROLLING - is a person who sows discord on the Internet by starting quarrels or upsetting people, by posting inflammatory, extraneous, or off-topic messages in an online community with the intent of provoking readers into an emotional response or of otherwise disrupting normal, on-topic discussion, often for the troll's amusement.
